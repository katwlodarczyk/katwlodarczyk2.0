### I have grown and learned a lot this past year.

I have been more thoughtful about stuff I surround myself with, realising that the less clutter = less stress and mental overload. **Quality > quantity, guys.**

At the beginning of the year I splurged on a standing desk (thanks to my boss's recommendation I still made a good deal as some standing desks are pretty expensive). This desk is still one of the best buys of the year. I spend most of my days at the desk, and to be able to switch from sitting to standing, or simply adjust the height to best suit my current activity is a godsend for my back.
