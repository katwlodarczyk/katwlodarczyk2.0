## Completing the Final Chapter

Finally got to process and sit down to write the summary of the third year at uni — the last leg of this great, life-changing journey that university has become. After graduating I jumped into full-time employment, which with the addition of being a parent, leaves no room for much more extra activities. Although I was super happy to finally see the finish line, some part of me was a bit sad that this part of my life is ending.

## The Most Challenging Year Yet

The third year was by far the most challenging and the most rewarding at the same time. It included a final major project, a dissertation, and several other demanding units — all while working part-time.

## The Final Major Project

My FMP was [Haircare Scan](haircare-scan-vue3-pwa), a Vue3 PWA application that helps users identify suitable hair care products by scanning ingredient lists. It became one of the projects I'm most proud of.

## Academic Curriculum

The third year covered a wide range of topics: advanced JavaScript, UX research, and professional practice. Each unit pushed me to think more critically about the code and design decisions I make.

## Graduation & New Beginnings

And here I am, a Bachelor of Science and a web developer. Some people say going to uni or a degree is not worth much anymore, but it opened the door to a life and a career I've dreamed about. I'd say it's very much worth it!
